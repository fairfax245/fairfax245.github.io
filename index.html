About Me

I am a literary scholar specializing in postmodern narratives, Gothic literature, and contemporary cultural studies. With a Ph.D. in English from Boston University, my research focuses on literary theory, comparative literature, and critical analysis. I am passionate about exploring the intersections of literature and culture, and I have contributed to academic discourse through publications and conference presentations.

My work examines how contemporary narratives reflect and shape cultural identities, with particular attention to the evolution of literary forms and their social implications.
